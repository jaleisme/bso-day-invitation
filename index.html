Capture the moment and let your creativity shine at our lively photobooth. Props-up, strike a pose, and create memories that will last a lifetime!

And that's not all! Brace yourself for many more surprises and delights that will unfold throughout the day.
BSO Day 2023 is not just an event; it's an experience designed to celebrate progress, foster connections, and create a better BSO environment.
Join us for a day of discovery, laughter, and celebration. Your presence will make BSO Day 2023 truly special! See you there!
